Domain & Research Details

Literature Survey

Agricultural technology affects around 30% of the global farming population, addressing challenges with soil degradation, water scarcity, and crop yield optimization. Conventional methods often lack scalability and real-time data, which restricts accessibility and innovation in farming practices.

Research Problem

Agricultural challenges are characterized by inefficiencies in resource use, unpredictable weather patterns, and limited access to advanced tools despite normal farming expertise. These issues stem from underlying challenges in data integration and technology adoption, particularly the disconnect between traditional farming knowledge and modern solutions. Farmers often face difficulties in adopting new technologies, requiring resource-intensive training and expert supervision. Technology offers a promising avenue to support farmers by providing accessible, engaging, and personalized solutions. Our project fills this gap.

Technologies Used

Our solution leverages IoT sensors for real-time soil and weather monitoring, AI algorithms for crop disease detection, and cloud-based platforms for data analysis. These technologies enable precise farming practices, optimize resource use, and provide actionable insights to farmers.
